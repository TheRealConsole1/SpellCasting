Countries

  • Lumira

    Lumira is the place that our heroes begin their journey. It is a lnad of magic and fantasy creatures, where humans are all but extinct. We don't know much about this place in the current moment, though this land uses a monarchy style of government, where kings and queens rule the land. Currently there are only two places we know about (which will both be described in the cities section) Newhaven, and Lakestone Shores.

  • America

    America is a country in the western hemisphere and is known for its diverse culture, economy, and political influence. It is where our heroes start their journey.

Cities

  • Lakestone Shores

    Lakestone Shores is a port city within Lumira and is also where our adventurers begin their journey. it is a vast city known for its wide variety of goods and services, as well as it's bustling harbor. The town is known for its medieval architecture and bustling streets, where merchants sit day and night selling goods. Every corner has its own collection of taverns. inns, brothels and more.
