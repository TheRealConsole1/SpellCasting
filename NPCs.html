NPCs

Here is a list of NPCs that we have met so far in the campaign, along with their information! Keep in mind, this information is strictly canon, and is known by our characters. If any of this information is wrong, conflicting with notes, or outdated, please let me know.

  • Crown Princess Reagan

    • Reagan is the rightful heir to the throne and is known for her kindness and wisdom. She is the current queens daughter, and is to inheret the crown once her father, King Jaeheres, passes. She is an aasimar, as is the rest of the royal family. She was one of the first people our adventurers met, telling everyone around her that she sensed something was happening within Lakestone Shores, though she doesn't know what draws her to there (until the adventurers show up) Long ago, Reagan was threatened by her half sibling, Michael, who was jealous of her position as heir to the throne. A great war broke out between the two, though in the end, Michael was cast down, and disowned by his family. He was cursed by Reagan to never be able to draw his full power again, and cast away to NewHaven. She believes that the adventurers are key in a Prophecy that her and her family believe, and so once they showed up, she helped train them in the ways of her world, including using magic and battle. She has since returned home, to do what, no one knows.

  • Michael

    • Michael is an aasimar and is the half sibling of Reagan, and is also a total bitch. Ever since he found out that Reagan was to be the next heir to the throne he has done nothing short of treason in trying to kill her and take her place with massive armies and relentless battle, a war was waged, but in the end, Michael was not able to kill the crown princess, and instead got a curse put on him that binds his power, however, despite this, Michael still ended up burning down Lumira and Newhaven, and ended up burning the castle down. Now, Michael found out about the new "people" in the realm, and, sensing an opportunity to seize his power back and break this curse offers them a deal, break the curse and they can leave with their lives. Aaron took this deal after some deliberation from the rest of the group. Upon agreeing to the deal, Aarons arm immediately looked to be wrapped in a sort of fabric and was hot to the touch. Later, after some trickery, they used Dispel Magic to break Michaels curse. Once the curse was broken, Michael dissapeared, and hasn't been seen since.

  • Mila

    • Mila is one of the mistresses daughters and is 8 years old. She is a curious little girl, spending most of her time either in the brothel or exploring the city. She befriended the adventurers when Alex started working in the brothel, and has been sent to send Alex and Aaron news about their jobs inside the brothel.
